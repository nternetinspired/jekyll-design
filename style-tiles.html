For more information on Style tiles I recommend reading this excellent ALA article, by their creator, Samantha Warren: Style Tiles and How They Work.

To create a new style style tile simply duplicate and re-name the file: tiles/_posts/2014-09-04-style-tile-default.html then go ahead and apply the styles in your css. The page title is automatically added to the tiles container class, so you can scope your styles easily. See _sass/views/_style-tile-1.scss for an example of scoped styling.
